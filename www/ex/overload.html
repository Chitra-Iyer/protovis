Property Precedence

Dot


Here left takes priority over right, and top over bottom.

Bar


Here left and width take priority over left and right, while top and height take priority over top and bottom.

Inheritance


Inheritance does not have any special role in property precedence. Even though the second dot in the first example only specifies the bottom and right properties, because it inherits left and top, those take priority. Generally speaking, you should stick to one coordinate system for positioning (at least within one inheritance tree) to avoid this ambiguity. Alternatively, as in the second example, you can set properties to null to prevent them from being inherited.
